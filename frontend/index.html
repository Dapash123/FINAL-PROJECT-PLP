Reduce Food Waste. Feed Communities.

Problem: Small farmers and food producers often lose income due to surplus or "imperfect" produce that doesn’t reach markets and exploitation by middlemen. Meanwhile, communities face food insecurity.

Solution: HarvestHub is a mobile/web platform that uses AI and geolocation to connect small-scale farmers, grocery stores, and restaurants with surplus food to local NGOs, food banks, or community kitchens in real time.

How It Works

  • Farmers input surplus crops via app (with photo + location).
  • AI estimates quantity and shelf life using image recognition.
  • System matches with nearest pickup/distribution partner.
  • Low-cost logistics partners handle transport.
  • Commercial suppliers pay a 10% service fee on redistributed food (NGOs are free).
  • Backend: Python (Flask) – REST API
  • Database: MySQL
  • AI/ML: Hugging Face Transformers (Image-to-Text QA + Crop Insight Models)
  • Payments: Flutterwave, Paystack, or Paga API
  • Cloud Deployment: Render, Vercel, or similar
